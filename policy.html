📋 Official MoneyTalks Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

1. 🎯 Purpose of This Policy

This Official Policy document outlines the comprehensive rules, guidelines, and operational standards for the MoneyTalks platform. It serves as the authoritative reference for how our platform operates, how user accounts are managed, and what standards we maintain.

This policy works in conjunction with our Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and Earnings Disclaimer to provide complete transparency about our operations.

2. 👤 Account Policies

2.1 Account Creation

  • Users must be at least 18 years old to create an account
  • One account per person (no multiple accounts)
  • Accurate information must be provided during registration
  • Email verification is required for all accounts
  • Accounts are non-transferable

2.2 Account Security

  • Users are responsible for maintaining their password security
  • Two-factor authentication is recommended (coming soon)
  • Notify us immediately of any unauthorized access
  • We never ask for your password via email or message
  • Regular security audits are performed on all accounts

2.3 Account Suspension & Termination

  • Violation of terms may result in account suspension
  • Fraudulent activity leads to immediate permanent ban
  • Inactive accounts may be suspended after 12 months
  • Users may request account deletion at any time
  • Banned users forfeit any remaining credits or benefits

3. 💰 Earning Policies

3.1 Link Earnings

  • Earnings from third-party links are processed by those sites
  • MoneyTalks does not process or hold cryptocurrency
  • Each site has its own minimum payout thresholds
  • Earnings vary based on site rules and user activity
  • We track clicks but not actual earnings from third parties

3.2 Game Credits

  • Game credits purchased via PayPal/Razorpay are non-refundable
  • Credits can only be used for game spins
  • No cash value - credits cannot be withdrawn
  • Bonus credits from game wins are for entertainment only
  • Credits expire after 12 months of inactivity

3.3 Referral Program

  • Referral credits are awarded when referred users purchase plans
  • Self-referrals are strictly prohibited
  • Referral fraud results in immediate account termination
  • Referral bonuses are credited within 24 hours
  • Maximum referral earnings are unlimited (fair play only)

4. 💳 Payment Policies

4.1 Plan Purchases

  • All plan purchases are final and non-refundable
  • Payments processed via PayPal (international) or Razorpay (India)
  • Plan features activate immediately upon successful payment
  • Prices are in USD (converted to local currency by payment processors)
  • We do not store payment information on our servers

4.2 Refund Policy

  • No refunds for change of mind
  • Technical issues preventing access may qualify for refund within 7 days
  • Refund requests must be submitted via email
  • Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days
  • Payment processor fees are non-refundable

4.3 Chargebacks

  • Chargebacks result in immediate account suspension
  • Disputed transactions are investigated thoroughly
  • False chargebacks may result in legal action
  • Accounts with chargebacks lose all access and benefits
  • Reinstatement requires full payment plus fees

5. 🎮 Game Policies

5.1 Fair Play

  • All game outcomes are determined by random number generation
  • Exploiting bugs or glitches is strictly prohibited
  • Automated scripts or bots are not allowed
  • Multiple accounts to gain extra spins is forbidden
  • Fair play monitoring is active on all games

5.2 Spin Mechanics

  • Each spin costs $0.25 from credit balance
  • Win rates: 60% lose, 20% site win, 20% cash win
  • Site wins provide affiliate links (real earning opportunities)
  • Cash wins add bonus credits to balance (for entertainment)
  • Spin history is recorded for all users

5.3 Cheating Prevention

  • DevTools detection is active
  • Abnormal click patterns are monitored
  • VPN/proxy abuse is tracked
  • Cheaters are permanently banned
  • Cheat logs are reviewed by administrators

6. 🔒 Privacy & Data Policies

6.1 Data Collection

  • Email addresses for account creation and communication
  • Username for public profile identification
  • Payment information (not stored by us, processed by PayPal/Razorpay)
  • Game activity for fair play monitoring
  • IP addresses for security and fraud prevention
  • Device information for platform optimization

6.2 Data Usage

  • Account management and customer support
  • Platform improvement and feature development
  • Security monitoring and fraud prevention
  • Communication about updates and promotions
  • Legal compliance and regulatory requirements

6.3 Data Protection

  • All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
  • Firebase security rules protect database access
  • Regular security audits performed
  • No selling of user data to third parties
  • Data retained only as long as necessary

7.1 Absolutely Forbidden:

  • Multiple Accounts: Creating more than one account
  • Automation: Using bots, scripts, or automated tools
  • Fraud: Fake referrals, payment fraud, or chargeback abuse

  • Abuse: Harassing other users or staff
  • Illegal Activity: Money laundering or illegal transactions
  • Impersonation: Pretending to be someone else
  • Scraping: Copying content without permission

7.2 Consequences of Violations

  • First offense: Warning (for minor issues)
  • Second offense: Temporary suspension
  • Severe violations: Permanent ban without warning
  • Fraud: Immediate permanent ban and legal action possible
  • Banned users forfeit all credits and plan benefits
